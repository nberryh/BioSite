She completed a field camp in Ireland for the earth and oceanographic sciences, which allowed her to receive her degree and start her enjoyment of being in the field, finding the secret that lay beneath the Earth’s Crust. Additionally, she has become an expert in field work which requires her to go out into a field of interest, collect and examine samples for her company, and report back her findings. Other necessary fieldwork she was included in was researching the impact of an oil spill in the Gulf of Mexico and conducting experiments on samples collected from the Depp-Water Horizon Oil Spill. With these studies, she was able to hone her skills in analyzing statistical data using both modern and traditional methods.
